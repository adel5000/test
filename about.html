About CityPulse

Connecting Communities Through Extraordinary Events

CityPulse is Syria's premier event discovery platform, dedicated to bringing people together through cultural, educational, and entertainment experiences.

Our Mission

To create a vibrant ecosystem where event organizers and attendees connect seamlessly, fostering cultural exchange and community engagement across Syria.

  • Bridge the gap between event organizers and the community
  • Promote cultural diversity and artistic expression
  • Support local businesses and creative industries
  • Make event discovery accessible to everyone

Our Vision

To become Syria's most trusted event platform, transforming how people discover, share, and participate in community events while preserving and promoting our rich cultural heritage.

  • Digital transformation of Syria's event industry
  • National platform connecting all Syrian cities
  • Innovation in event technology and user experience
  • Sustainable community growth and development

Why CityPulse Exists

In a country rich with cultural diversity and historical significance, CityPulse was born from a simple need: to create a centralized hub where Syrians can discover and participate in events that matter to them.

We believe that events have the power to:

  • Strengthen community bonds and social cohesion
  • Preserve and celebrate Syrian cultural heritage
  • Support economic recovery through tourism and local business
  • Provide platforms for artistic and creative expression
  • Offer educational and personal development opportunities

Event Submission Policies

Submission Requirements
  • Events must be open to the public
  • Clear event description and objectives
  • Accurate date, time, and location information
  • Appropriate categorization and tagging
  • Contact information for event organizers
  • High-quality images representing the event
Review Process
  • All events are manually reviewed within 24-48 hours
  • Verification of event details and organizer credibility
  • Compliance check with community guidelines
  • Quality assessment of event information
  • Notification of approval or required modifications

Publishing Standards & Guidelines

Content Quality

Events must provide clear, accurate, and complete information with high-quality visuals.

Community Respect

All events must respect cultural diversity and promote inclusive, safe environments.

Legal Compliance

Events must comply with Syrian laws and regulations, including necessary permits.

Important Notice

We reserve the right to reject or remove events that don't meet our standards, contain misleading information, or violate our community guidelines. Repeated violations may result in organizer account suspension.
